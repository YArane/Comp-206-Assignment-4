WELCOME TO CARTRIDGEBOOK

Many websites specialize in ink cartridges, but there's always some kind of transaction fee involved. Where do you go when all you want to do is simply admire these smooth pieces of plastic filled with hot ink? You come to cartridge-book, a community filled with normal people, just like you.

Whether you're interested in thick black cartridges filled with ink, or even smaller yellow HP-11s, you'll find a safe space to discuss them here with friends.
